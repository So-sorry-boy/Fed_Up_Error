Resolving Black Screen Errors in Windows

Applies to: Windows 11, Windows 10 | Last Updated: July 7, 2025 | Article ID: KB-FED-UP-777

Typical image of the "FED UP FROM EVERYTHING" stop error.

  • Remove emotionally draining tasks from startup.
  • Boot into safe mode: blanket + snacks + playlist.
  • Free storage by deleting guilt logs and regret files.
  • Update your attitude drivers regularly.
  • Run the Self Diagnostics Tool (journaling, therapy).
  • Clear cache of what others think of you.
  • Apply patches like kindness, sleep, and perspective.
